ABOUT US

Guiding Students Towards Smarter Career Decisions

Get Set Admit is an AI-powered career guidance and university discovery platform designed for students in Classes 9, 10, 11, and 12.

We help students understand their interests, strengths, and future opportunities before they choose a course or university. Through intelligent assessments, expert guidance, and access to trusted institutions, students can confidently plan their higher education journey.

Whether you're exploring undergraduate programs in India or planning to study abroad, Get Set Admit simplifies the process and helps you make informed decisions.
